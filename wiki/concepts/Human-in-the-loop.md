# Human-in-the-loop

## Definition

Human-in-the-loop describes workflows where people review, guide, approve, or correct automated work.

## Why It Matters

It helps keep agentic systems aligned with intent, quality standards, and privacy boundaries.

## Key Ideas

- Approval gates are useful before irreversible changes.
- Humans should handle ambiguous goals and sensitive tradeoffs.
- Review artifacts should be easy to inspect.
